# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Casual Solana users who heard they can reclaim locked SOL from empty token accounts. Not technical. They want the process to feel obvious, safe, and worth their time. They arrive wondering "is this legit?" and need to trust the tool before clicking anything.

## Product Purpose

Close empty SPL token accounts on Solana and return the locked rent deposit (~0.00203 SOL each) to the user's wallet. The tool exists because Solana locks rent in every token account, and most users don't know they can get it back. Success looks like: user connects wallet, sees exactly what they'll recover, clicks one button, and gets their SOL back in seconds.

## Brand Personality

Friendly, helpful, transparent. The tone is a knowledgeable friend who shows you a trick to save money, not a salesperson. Direct about fees. No hype.

## Anti-references

- Overly dark "hacker" terminal UIs with neon green text and monospace everything
- Cluttered DeFi interfaces with dozens of stats, buttons, and panels competing for attention
- Over-designed crypto pages with particle effects, excessive animations, and gradient text
- Generic SaaS metric dashboards with big hero numbers

## Design Principles

1. **Show the money first.** The user came to reclaim SOL. Show them how much they can get back before asking them to do anything.
2. **One path, no maze.** Connect → scan → review → close. No tabs, no settings, no modes. The interface is a single linear flow.
3. **Honest about costs.** The fee tier is visible before the transaction, not buried. Trust comes from transparency.
4. **Calm confidence.** The interface should feel like it knows what it's doing. No exclamation marks, no urgency tricks, no "hurry" language. Competent and steady.
5. **Nothing extra.** Every element earns its place. If removing something doesn't hurt comprehension, remove it.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Readable text with sufficient contrast. Light motion is acceptable. No strict WCAG level requirement, but body text should meet at least 4.5:1 contrast ratio against its background.
